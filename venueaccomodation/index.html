Venue

The PhD school will be held at Bielefeld University.

Accomodation

Students and lecturers will at Mercure Hotel Bielefeld Johannisberg, where we will also have breakfast and dinner together. Close to the hotel, there are lots of opportunities for leisure activities, and the city center is in walking distance.

Transfer

The distance from the hotel to Bielefeld University is about 3 km. We will organize a bus shuttle for the transfer. In case of nice weather, we may also take a nice hike through the forest from the university to the hotel in the afternoon.
