Application and Registration

In total, the PhD school will have up to 30 student participants. To apply, please send an application email with

  • a short academic CV
  • your PhD topic
  • you accomodation preference (single room or sharing a double room)

    • to michael.roemer@uni-bielefeld.de until July 31, 2023.

    Given the generous support from Euro, GOR and Bielefeld University, the cost for each participant, covering the participation in school, social acititivities, food (including breakfast, lunch and dinner) and accomodation in Mercure Hotel Bielefeld Johannisberg the can be kept relatively moderate:

  • Single room: ≤ € 300
  • Sharing a double room (separate beds) ≤ € 160

    • The exact costs and the mode of payment will be announced once the registration is complete.
